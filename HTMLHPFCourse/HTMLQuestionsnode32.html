Solution

  • A(12) -- OK,
  • A(21) -- out of bounds,
  • A(I) -- OK (is A(3)),
  • A(3.0) -- cannot subscript by REAL,
  • A(I*J) -- out of bounds (A(21)),
  • A(1+INT(4.0*ATAN(1.0))) -- OK, 1+INT(4.0*ATAN(1.0)) = 4

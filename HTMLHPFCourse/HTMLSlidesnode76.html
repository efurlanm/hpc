Array Sections - Visualisation

Given, 

    REAL, DIMENSION(1:6,1:8) :: P

Consider the following assignments,

  • P(1:3,1:4) = P(::2,::2) and
    P(1:3,1:4) = 1.0 are valid.
  • P(2::2,:7:3) = P(1:3,1:4) and
    P(2::2,:7:3) = P(2:5,7) are not.
  • P(2:5,7) is a 1D section whereas
    P(2:5,7:7) is a 2D section

Aligning Scalars

HPF does not forbid mapping scalars [yet]: 

     REAL, DIMENSION(6,8) :: X
     REAL r, s
 !HPF$ ALIGN r WITH X(3,2)
 !HPF$ ALIGN s WITH X(*,8)

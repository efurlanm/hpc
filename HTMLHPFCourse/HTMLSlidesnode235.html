Problems with Modules

HPF objects cannot appear in ONLY or renames lists in a USE statement, for example, 

    SUBROUTINE Subby(A,B,RES)
     USE Global_Mapping_Info, ONLY:ProcArr => P

is invalid Fortran 90.

Procedure Classes

A Fortran 90 program must contain a main program, it can invoke other user-defined procedures which may be:

  • external -- external to the procedure which calls them, can be compiled separately.
  • internal -- contained within the procedure or main program that calls them (called the host).
  • module-procedures -- have some characteristics of both internal and external procedures (see later).

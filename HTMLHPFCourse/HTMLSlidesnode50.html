Examples of Loop Counts

A few examples of different loops,

  1. upper bound not exact, 
        loopy: DO i = 1, 30, 2  
     ... ! 15 iterations
    END DO loopy
  2. negative stride, 
        DO j = 30, 1, -2 
     ... ! 15 iterations
    END DO
  3. a zero-trip loop, 
        DO k = 30, 1, 2  
     ... ! 0 iterations
     ... ! loop skipped
    END DO
  4. missing stride, 
        DO l = 30,1
     ... ! zero-trip loop
    END DO

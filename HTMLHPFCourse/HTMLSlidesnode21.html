Comments

It is good practise to use lots of comments: 

    INTEGER schminteger       ! comment here
    !HPF$ PROCESSORS P(3,3,3) ! HPF directive

  • everything after the ! is a comment;
  • the ! in a character context does not begin a comment;
  • many compilation systems interpret formatted comments, for example, HPF systems.

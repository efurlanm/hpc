Strided Alignment Example

Align each element of D with every second element of E: 

    REAL, DIMENSION(5)  :: D
    REAL, DIMENSION(10) :: E
!HPF$ ALIGN D(:) WITH E(1::2)

This says: i, elements D(i) and E(i*2-1) are aligned. For example, D(3) and E(5). Alignment could also be written: 

!HPF$ ALIGN D(i) WITH E(i*2-1)

This alignment is suitable for, 

 D = D + E(::2) ! All local

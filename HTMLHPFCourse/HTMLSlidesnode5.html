Fortran Evolution

History:

  • FORmula TRANslation.
  • first compiler: 1957.
  • first official standard 1972: `FORTRAN 66'.
  • updated in 1980 to FORTRAN 77.
  • updated further in 1991 to Fortran 90.
  • next upgrade due in 1995 - remove obsolescent features, correct mistakes and add limited basket of new facilities such as ELEMENTAL and PURE user-defined procedures and the FORALL statement.
  • Fortran is now an ISO/IEC and ANSI standard.

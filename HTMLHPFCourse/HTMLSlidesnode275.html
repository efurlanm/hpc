Further Example of Array Sorting Functions

If A is the 2D array: 

    1 9 2
    4 5 2
    1 2 4

Then GRADE_DOWN(A) is (the coordinates) 

    1 2 2 3 3 1 2 1 3
    2 2 1 3 2 3 3 1 1

and GRADE_DOWN(A,DIM=1) is 

    2 1 3
    1 2 1
    3 3 2
